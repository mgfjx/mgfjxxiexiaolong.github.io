1、Xcode 8.3无法使用PackageApplication

提示：xcrun: error: unable to find utility "PackageApplication", not a developer tool or in PATH.
解决办法: 从Xcode 8.2拷贝PackageApplication到当先Xcode对应目录
1) 下载PackageApplication:https://gist.github.com/anonymous/48f3e4c5ae25313dc0fe10d9ec50c3fc
2) 修改属性为可执行: chmod +x PackageApplication
3) 拷贝到对应目录: /Applications/Xcode.app/Contents/Developer/Platforms/iPhoneOS.platform/Developer/usr/bin/PackageApplication
